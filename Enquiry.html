🤔 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of pets do you accept?
A: We welcome all types of pets! Dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, hamsters, guinea pigs, and even exotic pets. Our experienced team is trained to handle pets of all sizes and temperaments with specialized care for each species.
Q: Do I need to book in advance?
A: While we accept walk-ins when possible, we highly recommend booking in advance to guarantee your preferred time slot. Popular services like grooming and massage therapy typically require 2-3 days advance notice.
Q: What should I bring when dropping off my pet?
A: Please bring your pet's vaccination records, any special medications, their favorite toy or blanket for comfort, and detailed feeding instructions. We'll provide everything else they need!
Q: Are your staff members qualified?
A: Absolutely! Our team includes certified veterinary technicians, professional groomers, animal behaviorists, and trained pet care specialists. All staff undergo continuous training and background checks.
Q: What if my pet has special dietary needs?
A: No problem! We accommodate all dietary restrictions, allergies, and medical conditions. Just provide us with detailed instructions, and we'll ensure your pet receives exactly what they need.
Q: Can I visit my pet during their stay?
A: While we prefer to minimize disruptions during care sessions, scheduled visits can be arranged. We also provide regular updates and photos to keep you connected with your pet's experience!
